# Experiment 03 - Comparison with the Hur et al. 2022 Paper

## Overview
In April 2026, comparison scripts will be developed to evaluate QCNN implementations against the Hur et al. 2022 paper. The analysis will focus on scalability, execution speed, and accuracy across different simulators.

## Key Objectives
- Develop comparison scripts for QCNN implementations
- Evaluate scalability and execution speed
- Compare accuracy across different simulators
- Generate graphical visualizations of results

## Deliverables
- Comparative analysis with Hur et al. 2022 QCNN implementations
- Performance metrics and benchmarks
- Visualizations using Matplotlib or similar libraries
